Data from 

Lefferts, W. K, DeBlois, J. P., White, C. N., Day, T. A., Heffernan, K. S., & Brutsaert, T. D. (2019).  Changes in cognitive function and latent processes of decision-making during incremental ascent to high altitude.  Physiology & Behavior, 201, 139-145.


6 subjects who did 15 separate sessions -- 11 practice and then 12-15 at each of 4 base camps at Everest (increasing in altitude)


Data Format:

Block and trials

Congr -- compatibility condition (1 = compatible, 2 = incompatible)

Stim -- Target arrow faces (1 = left, 2 = right)

Resp -- button press (1 = left, 2 = right)

	So correct responses are when [stim == 1 & resp ==1] or [stim ==2 & resp==2]

RT (in seconds)
